How do I know if my roof has hail damage?

Look for dents or bruises on shingles, cracked or missing granules, and damage to gutters, vents, or siding. Our team offers a free inspection to assess any hail-related damage.

Do I need to contact my insurance company first?

No — schedule a free inspection with us first. We'll document the damage and guide you through the insurance claims process step by step.

Will my insurance cover the roof replacement?

In most cases, yes. If the damage is caused by a covered peril like hail or wind, your insurance typically covers the replacement minus your deductible.

Will filing a hail claim raise my insurance rates?

Hail claims are typically classified as "acts of nature" and generally do not raise your premium. This varies by provider and state.

What will this cost me out of pocket?

If your claim is approved, your only cost is typically your insurance deductible. We handle the rest directly with your insurance company.

How long does the inspection and roof replacement process take?

The inspection takes about 30–45 minutes. Once approved, roof replacement is typically completed in 1–3 days.

What if my insurance company denies the claim?

We'll review the denial and assist with filing a supplement or appeal. We also offer financing options if you choose to move forward out of pocket.
